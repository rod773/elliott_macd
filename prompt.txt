==============================================================
ADDITIONAL GENERATION NOTES FOR META AI
==============================================================

1. CONSISTENCY: Maintain the same chart style, color palette,
   and font throughout all scenes. Do not switch visual themes.

2. TRANSITIONS: Use smooth crossfades or slide transitions between
   scenes. Avoid hard cuts on educational content.

3. ANNOTATIONS: All chart annotations (wave labels, divergence lines,
   entry/exit markers) should animate onto the screen sequentially,
   not appear all at once.

4. AUDIO: Narrator voice should be consistent. Use the same voice
   actor/style from intro to outro. Music fades slightly when
   narrator speaks and rises in transitions.

5. PACING: Each module should feel complete — do not rush. Allow
   2–4 seconds after key concepts before advancing.

6. CHAPTER MARKERS: Add YouTube-style chapter timestamps at:
   0:00 Intro | 0:30 Module 1 | 4:00 Module 2 | 7:30 Module 3
   11:00 Module 4 | 16:00 Module 5 | 20:00 Module 6
   23:00 Module 7 | 26:00 Module 8 | 30:00 Outro

7. LOWER THIRDS: Show module title as a lower-third graphic when
   each new module begins (cyan accent, dark background bar).

8. DISCLAIMER: Show at intro and outro:
   "For educational purposes only. Not financial advice."
